## 📌 Overview

We're building a web app to help hikers easily find up-to-date Pacific Northwest trail information. The site will detail trail difficulty, conditions, and closures to support hiker safety, and grow a community around responsible outdoor recreation.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Deploy MVP website by **June 12**
- Deliver fully functional MVP features
- Gather user reviews to measure usability

**Success looks like:** 100+ positive reviews in the first month, no critical errors after release, and useful feedback for future features.

## ✅ Scope

**In Scope:**
- Desktop web browser support
- Simple, reliable design with clean web pages and forms
- Trail search bar with filters (difficulty, length, region, etc.)
- Posts, forums, and reply sections for user communication

**Out of Scope:**
- Mobile app
- User accounts and favorites
- GPS tracking and live weather/trail conditions
