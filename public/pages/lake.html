Puget Sound & Whidbey Island

Explore Deception Pass State Park, the Bluff Trail, and Fort Ebey State Park. Features WWII bunkers and ocean views.

Difficulty: Easy Distance: 3 miles Elevation Gain: 200 ft

Distance 3 miles loop
Elevation Gain 200 ft
Trail Type Lakeside path
Best For Relaxation, photography, birdwatching

What to Expect

Calm water views, gentle shoreline walking, and easy access points. Expect a mix of boardwalk and gravel surfaces, with good options for picnics and water watching.

Highlights

  • Lakeside viewpoints
  • Wildlife and waterfowl
  • Easy, flat walking

Plan Ahead

  • Bring a picnic and sun protection
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Watch for slippery sections near the water
