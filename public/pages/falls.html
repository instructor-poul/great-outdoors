Franklin Falls

An easy 2-mile roundtrip trail that follows a rushing river to a picturesque, 70-foot tiered waterfall.

Difficulty: Easy Distance: 2 miles Elevation Gain: 300 ft

Distance 2 miles roundtrip
Elevation Gain 300 ft
Trail Type Out-and-back
Best For Families, photographers, waterfalls

What to Expect

Gentle forested paths, a river crossing, and a final viewpoint with misting cascades. Expect moderate crowds on weekends and soft mud after rain.

Highlights

  • Waterfall viewpoint with easy access
  • Shaded trail sections
  • Family-friendly terrain

Plan Ahead

  • Bring water and sturdy shoes
  • Pack a rain shell for mist from the falls
  • Arrive early to avoid parking delays
