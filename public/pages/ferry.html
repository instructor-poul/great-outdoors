Olympic Peninsula

The High Divide and Seven Lakes Basin Loop (Olympic National Park) and the Ozette Triangle to Cape Alava on the coast.

Difficulty: Moderate Distance: 4 miles Elevation Gain: 150 ft

Distance 4 miles roundtrip
Elevation Gain 150 ft
Trail Type Loop / coastal walk
Best For Sightseeing, birds, photography

What to Expect

A blend of open water, shoreline vistas, and gentle boardwalk sections. Expect steady winds and wide views, with occasional ferry traffic on the horizon.

Highlights

  • Waterfront panoramas
  • Wildlife spotting opportunities
  • Easy access from nearby parking

Plan Ahead

  • Dress in layers for wind and spray
  • Carry binoculars for coastal birds
  • Bring sunscreen and a hat
