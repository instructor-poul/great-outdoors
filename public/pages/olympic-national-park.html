Olympic National Park

Experience diverse ecosystems from rugged coastlines to temperate rainforests in Olympic National Park.

Difficulty: Hard Distance: 7 miles Elevation Gain: 1,500 ft

Distance 7 miles roundtrip
Elevation Gain 1,500 ft
Trail Type Point-to-point
Best For Rainforest, coastline, wilderness hiking

What to Expect

A mixture of mossy forest, rugged coastline, and open ridgelines. Hikers should prepare for wet conditions, rocky sections, and changing weather.

Highlights

  • Coastal and rainforest ecosystems
  • Diverse wildlife and plant life
  • Remote backcountry feel

Plan Ahead

  • Bring rain gear and waterproof boots
  • Check tide and weather forecasts
  • Carry extra food and emergency supplies
