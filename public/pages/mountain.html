Skyline Trail Loop

A premier 5.5-mile loop at Paradise with massive summer wildflower blooms and jaw-dropping mountain views. Ideal for experienced hikers.

Difficulty: Hard Distance: 5.5 miles Elevation Gain: 1,300 ft

Distance 5.5 miles loop
Elevation Gain 1,300 ft
Trail Type Loop
Best For Wildflowers, alpine views, experienced hikers

What to Expect

Steady climbs and open alpine ridges with sweeping views of glaciers and mountain peaks. Expect exposed sections, rocky tread, and strong sun at higher elevations.

Highlights

  • Epic mountain panoramas
  • Wildflower meadows in summer
  • High-alpine exposure and ridgelines

Plan Ahead

  • Bring layers for wind and cold
  • Carry extra water and sunscreen
  • Use trekking poles on descent
