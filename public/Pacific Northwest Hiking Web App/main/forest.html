Forest

A forest is a large, complex ecosystem dominated by trees, undergrowth, and a vast network of living organisms. Forests are far more than just collections of individual trees; they are highly integrated biological communities where the roots, soil, plants, wildlife, and fungi all depend on one another to survive.
